About

I'm an Australian chartered tax adviser and public accountant with international tax and crypto accounting expertise, working with creative and tech enterprises across borders since 2000. As a pioneer adopter of everything digital within reach, I've operated my practice in Sydney and remotely since 2008, putting Bitcoin on the balance sheet since 2013.

I believe the accounting profession has a critical role in stewarding public trust as society adapts to frontier technologies like AI, Bitcoin and decentralised finance. My work is dedicated to advancing leadership at this intersection through living and learning in public.

I was recently appointed Deputy President of Malaysia's Institute of Public Accountants Member Advisory Committee. My role supports IPA Global's member engagement strategy and GCPA transdisciplinary accounting qualification in Southeast Asia.

With this complementary focus, I'm embedded in Network School (Johor–Singapore SEZ) since the original 2024 cohort, designing a network state aligned professional accounting faculty and association, and drafting a Global Competencies Framework for a Bitcoin Standard.
